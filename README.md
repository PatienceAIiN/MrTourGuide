# Mr.Tour Guide — feel the world from home

**Mr.Tour Guide** is an accessibility-first virtual-travel app by **PatienceAI**.
It lets anyone — especially elderly, disabled, and home-bound travelers —
*feel* a place, not just watch it: real travel videos paired with phone
**haptics** (touch you can feel), **MR/VR** immersive modes, and an **AI trip
planner**. Creators share experiences; travelers explore them.

> Website: https://mrtourguide.patienceai.in · Android app (free)

---

## What you can do

### Feel real places
- Watch curated destination experiences that turn a video's sound and motion
  into **touch you can feel** — wind, footsteps, waves, a passing train.
- Adjust the **feel intensity** to your own comfort with a single slider.
- Switch on **MR / VR** for an immersive, headset-ready view.

### GuideVibe — short travel videos
- A full-screen, swipe-up feed of short travel clips (Reels/Shorts style),
  each with the signature feel-as-you-watch haptics.
- Creators upload or record vertical shorts, choose **Normal / VR / MR**, add a
  caption and city, and share.
- Like, comment, and share any short with a direct link.

### Plan with AI
- Ask the built-in **AI planner** for a day-by-day itinerary ("3 days in
  Rajasthan, wheelchair-friendly") and refine it in plain conversation.
- Save plans to your profile and revisit them anytime.
- AI search gives a clean overview of any place — how it feels, how to get
  there, where to stay, the best time to go, and practical tips.

### Community
- A friendly travel community: post photos and short videos, react
  Facebook-style, reply in threads, follow creators, and reshare.
- **#hashtags** are tappable everywhere and jump straight to search.

### Discover
- Live destination covers, weather, ratings and reviews on each place page.
- Local, city-aware **travel news** so you always know what's happening.

### Built for everyone
- Accessibility first: adjustable feel, reduce-motion mode, light & dark
  themes, and a clean, high-contrast interface.
- Sign in with email or Google; your data and preferences sync to your account.

---

## Who it's for
- Travelers who are elderly, disabled, unwell, or simply can't travel right now.
- Students and dreamers who want to experience the world before they go.
- Creators who want to share the *feeling* of a place, not just footage.
